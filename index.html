§ 02 — Practice

Four practices, one discipline.

We work with a deliberately small number of boards, cabinets and executive teams each year — on the decisions that don't yet have a clean dataset behind them, and the institutions that have to make them anyway.

01

Strategy & direction

Multi-month engagements with leadership teams on the single decision that matters most — where to point the institution in the next five years, and what to stop doing tomorrow.

Practice 02

Applied intelligence

The adoption of machine intelligence in the parts of the business where the cost of being wrong is real — risk, operations, regulated decisions, customer outcomes.

Practice 03

Expeditions & short briefs

A senior team for a single, time-bound question — a deal week, a board offsite, a crisis response. We arrive prepared, work on-site, and leave a written report behind.

Practice 04

Standing advisory

A long-running, low-noise relationship with a small number of CEOs and boards. Quarterly sessions, on-call between, and an annual written review.
